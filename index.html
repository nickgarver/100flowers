Enter the garden

100 Flowers is a ritual of return, a sanctuary of rhythm where community, connection, and the pulse of our shared humanity take root. Inspired by the beautiful native flowers here in Michigan, each bloom shows the resilience of life that is often overlooked.

Each show is centered around a different type of flower that embodies the dance floor we are all rooted to; our shared experience. The goal is to create a platform for friends to organically experiment with their music while blending into the soul of Detroit. We hope to let you be unhindered by the outside world and free to express yourselves.

In celebration of the spring season, we wanted to call this show Blossom. There's always sort of a stirring, explosive energy this time of year, and we hope you can feel this. We are grateful to share this moment with you.
