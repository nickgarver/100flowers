100 Flowers is a ritual of return, a sanctuary of rhythm where community, connection, and the pulse of our shared humanity take root. Originally it was inspired by the beautiful native flowers here in Michigan that I always see when walking outside; each bloom shows the resilience of life that is often passed by.

Each show is centered around a different type of flower to embody the dance floor we are all rooted to, our shared experience. My goal was to create a platform for friends to experiment with their music while blending into the soul of Detroit; I hope to allow the crowd to be unhindered by the outside word and be free to express yourself.

In celebration of the spring season, (one of my favorites) I wanted to call this show "blossom". There's always sort of a stirring / explosive energy this time of year and I hope you can feel this. Everything from ticket design, to the lighting, to the music we play is all created by friends and i am so excited to share everything with you.
